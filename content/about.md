# The Dart Family

We're the Darts — a family that loves adventures big and small, good food, and even better company. This blog is our little corner of the internet where we share what we're up to: road trips, backyard barbecues, school milestones, and everything in between.

## Our Family

We believe in slowing down enough to notice the good stuff — the way the kids laugh at their own jokes, the weekend hikes that turn into impromptu picnics, the dinners that go long because the conversation is too good to cut short.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Family updates** — the big moments and the everyday ones worth remembering
- **Photos** — snapshots from our life together
- **Stories** — because the details are what make it real

Thanks for stopping by. We're glad you're here.
