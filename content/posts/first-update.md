Summer arrived fast this year.

One week it was still jacket weather and school lunches, and the next — boom — school's out, the schedule dissolved, and somehow the backyard became the center of the universe.

## What We've Been Up To

**The garden is back.** After last year's tomato disaster (blight got them all by August), we tried again with better drainage and a lot more optimism. So far so good. The kids helped plant and have been very invested in daily status reports.

**A road trip is in the works.** We're keeping the destination under wraps until it's confirmed, but if it comes together it'll be the biggest trip we've taken as a family. More on that soon.

**Summer reading.** We started a family book list — one pick per person, everyone reads it, we talk about it over dinner. It sounds more organized than it is, but we're enjoying it.

## Looking Ahead

The rest of the summer looks open in the best possible way. A few smaller day trips, a lot of home time, and the general goal of not wasting any of it on things that don't matter.

We'll check back in as the summer unfolds.
