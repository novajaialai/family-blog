Welcome to Our Family Blog!

June 29, 2026 · 1 min

Well, we finally did it.

After years of scattered texts, buried Instagram posts, and group chats that somehow got titled “🏠 FAMILY” with zero irony, we decided to do something a little more intentional: start a family blog.

Why a Blog?#

A few reasons:

  1. Memory keeping. Photos live on phones until phones die. Words written down stick around longer.
  2. Sharing without algorithms. Not everything needs to be optimized for engagement. Some things are just for us — and for the people who care about us.
  3. The kids will thank us someday. (They won’t, probably, but we’ll feel good about it.)

What to Expect#

We’ll post updates when something worth sharing happens. That might be a big trip, a season wrap-up, a kid milestone, or just a funny thing that happened on a Tuesday. No schedule, no pressure — just honest updates when we have them.

Glad you’re here. More soon.

— The Darts
